Shortcuts:
w - move up
a - move left
s - move down
d - move right

After character you can write down the power of your move:
d5 - move right 5 times
w1 - move up 1 time
s10 - move down 10 times

The default power of move is 1


Example of the program:

Frame: 15. x: 7 y: 8
